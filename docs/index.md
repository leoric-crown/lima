# LIMA Documentation

> **LIMA** (Local Intelligent Memo Assistant) transforms voice recordings into structured notes using AI that runs entirely on your machine.

## Start Here

New to LIMA? Follow these guides in order:

### 1. [Getting Started](getting-started.md)
Set up LIMA and process your first voice memo. Takes about 15 minutes.

### 2. [Customizing Your AI](customizing-your-ai.md)
Choose and configure your local LLM (LM Studio or Ollama), adjust context windows, and tweak prompts.
